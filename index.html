Befriend a Senior

This is a youth-led project that connects seniors with youth volunteers through handwritten letters, care packages, conversations, and more. We aim to remind every senior they are seen, heard, and valued, while also giving students a space to grow, connect, and contribute.

Why This Matters

Many seniors face loneliness every day, especially being in the nursing home. Even small acts of kindness, like a thoughtful card or a fun visit can make a huge difference. By bringing together students and seniors, we create bonds that matter and build a stronger, more caring community.

What You’ll Gain

As a youth volunteer, you’ll grow your leadership skills, earn service hours, and connect with others. You'll also gain experience in teamwork, event planning, and giving back to the world around you.
